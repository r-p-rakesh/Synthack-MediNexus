Chronic Diseases

Learn about common chronic conditions and their symptoms

Diabetes

A chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy.

  • Increased thirst and urination
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing wounds
  • Unexplained weight loss

Heart Disease

A range of conditions that affect your heart's structure and function.

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Swelling in legs and feet
  • Fatigue and weakness

Kidney Disease

A condition where kidneys are damaged and can't filter blood properly.

  • Swelling in legs and ankles
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Changes in urination
  • High blood pressure
  • Loss of appetite

Breast Cancer

A type of cancer that forms in the cells of the breasts.

  • Lump in the breast
  • Changes in breast shape
  • Nipple discharge
  • Skin changes on breast
  • Breast pain

Asthma

A condition in which airways narrow and swell and produce extra mucus.

  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest tightness
  • Wheezing
  • Coughing
  • Trouble sleeping

Arthritis

Inflammation of one or more joints, causing pain and stiffness.

  • Joint pain
  • Stiffness
  • Swelling
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Redness around joints
